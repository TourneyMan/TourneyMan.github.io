I have decided to compare take a look at the Big Ten men's basketball teams. This scatterplot was made to determine if there is a correlation between the number of in-conference regular-season games that a team wins and well it does in 'March Madness', the NCAA Basketball tournament. Overall, this scatterplot shows a positive correlation between in-conference wins and tournament success. However, the correlation is a lot weaker than I would have expected.
